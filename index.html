Bulls and cows

Change difficulty

Rules

  • The computer guesses a random number from 3-6 (depends on the level of difficulty) different numbers.
  • You need to guess this number.
  • The computer displays the result (how many bulls, how many cows).
  • Example: guessed 1234, entered 1345, result: 1 bull (one is guessed in its place) and 2 cows (3 and 4 are present, but not in their places).
  • Everything is repeated until the user guesses the number.
